Olympics Track and Field Results (1896-2016)

The Olympics Games is a major international multi-sports competition, hosting over 200 teams represented by countries around the World. The atheletic festival originated in Athens, Greece, in 1896, and is traditionally held once every four years.

Track and field events is considered to be one of the earliest forms of athletics, and has been part of the Olympic events from the beginning.
